# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 10, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Grumma ("we", "our", or "us") is a Russian language learning web application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account information**: When you sign up, we collect your email address and generate a unique user ID
- **Authentication**: We use Supabase for authentication; your credentials are managed securely by their platform

### Learning Data
- **Exercise answers**: When you complete grammar exercises and spaced repetition reviews, we store your answers
- **Progress data**: We track your answers, timestamps, and correctness to power the spaced repetition learning system
- **Grammar points**: We record which grammar points you've practiced and your performance

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Russian language learning service
- Track your progress through grammar lessons
- Implement spaced repetition reviews based on your performance
- Send password reset and account verification emails (via Supabase)

## 4. Data Storage

- Your data is stored on Supabase (a PostgreSQL database service)
- Learning progress data is associated with your account to personalize your experience
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## 5. Your Rights

You may:
- Access your personal information
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Update your account information through the app

To delete your account, contact us at the email address listed on our contact page.

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Authentication is handled securely through Supabase.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact page on our website or via email grummaorg@proton.me.
