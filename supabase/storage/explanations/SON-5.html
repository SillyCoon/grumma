Instrumental Case - Complete Explanation

The instrumental case (творительный падеж) answers Кем? (By whom? As whom?) and Чем? (By what? With what?). It expresses the instrument, means, manner, or agent of an action.

Primary Functions

1. Tool or Instrument

Shows what tool or instrument is used to perform an action.

Я пишу ручкой. (I write with a pen.)
"ручкой" (pen) → instrumental case (tool/instrument)

2. Means of Transportation

How someone travels or moves.

Она ездит машиной. (She travels by car.)
Мы поехали поездом. (We traveled by train.)
"машиной", "поездом" → instrumental for transportation

3. Profession or Role

What someone works as or the role they have.

Она работает учителем. (She works as a teacher.)
Я хочу быть врачом. (I want to be a doctor.)
"учителем", "врачом" → instrumental for profession

4. Agency (Who Does Something)

In passive constructions, shows who performed the action.

Это письмо было написано поэтом. (This letter was written by a poet.)
"поэтом" (poet) → instrumental for agent in passive voice

5. After Certain Prepositions

Prepositions like с (with), перед (in front of), над (above), под (under) often require instrumental.

Я иду с другом. (I go with a friend.)
"другом" (friend) → instrumental after "с"

6. Manner or Method

How something is done or the manner of action.

Он говорит голосом смеха. (He speaks with a laughing voice.)
"голосом", "смеха" → instrumental for manner

Singular Endings

  • Masculine: -ом, -ем (стол → столом, день → днём)
  • Feminine: -ой, -ей (книга → книгой, земля → землей)
  • Neuter: -ом, -ем (окно → окном, море → морем)

Special Notes

• After soft consonants, use -ем, -ей, -ем instead of -ом, -ой, -ом
• After stems ending in ж, ш, ч, щ, use -ом, -ей
• Ending -ём is used for stressed syllables in masculine/neuter nouns

Common Patterns with "Быть"

Быть + instrumental: to be in a profession or role
"Она хочет быть врачом." (She wants to be a doctor.)
"Он был учителем." (He was a teacher.)

Practice Tips

  • Ask "with what?" "by what?" "as what?" to identify instrumental
  • Think of instrumental for tools, vehicles, professions
  • Remember soft consonant stems change to -ем, -ей
  • After "быть" + profession/role uses instrumental
  • Instrumental is often preceded by a preposition (с, над, перед, под)
